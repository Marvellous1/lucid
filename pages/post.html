Slack to allow employees work remotely for the remainder of this year.

What I have learned so far

I have learned a few things in the short period I've been around the community. I have picked up a few things from the pop quizzes that have been put out by the mentors. I have also been made to realise that having a community of people that share the same interests motivates to even work better. I have also learned more about slack in these few days than I've known in about three years now.

What I wish to learn

I wish to learn about web development and software engineering. I wish to learn the essentials when it comes to making a real life project. I also wish to know more about how to deploy applications. I also wish to learn more about back end programming.

What I wish should be improved

I wish information be disseminated in more compact forms in the groups so that there won't be confusion when someone comes later to read it all. I would also wish that stringent measures be taken on people who try to distort the order of any group, especially in groups that should solely be controlled by mentors.

All in all, it has been a wonderful experience thus far, I hope for better days ahead.

Cheers.
